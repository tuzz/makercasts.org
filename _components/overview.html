What is MakerCasts?

MakerCasts is a screencast series about making imaginative things with code. Episodes explore topics from computer science and mathematics from the perspective of a web developer.

I believe the best way to really understand something is to put theory into practice. In each series, we'll do just that by creating toy projects to play with, building intuition of the core ideas.

Is this for programmers?

Primarily, yes. It's aimed at inquisitive people, regardless of whether you're a curious beginner or a seasoned professional. Hopefully there's something here for everyone.

The emphasis is on breaking down complex ideas and explaining them in a constructive, easy-to-understand way. I want to give you the tools and the confidence to make something of your own.
