Comprehension through code

MakerCasts is a screencast series about making imaginative things with code. Episodes explore topics from computer science and mathematics, from the perspective of a web developer.

I believe the best way to really understand something is to put theory into practice. In each series, we'll do just that by creating toy projects to play with, gaining insight of the core ideas.

For inquisitive programmers

MakerCasts is aimed at curious coders, keen to learn more. Hopefully there's something here for everyone, regardless of whether you're just getting started or you've been programming for years.

The emphasis is on breaking down complex ideas and explaining them in a constructive, easy-to-understand way. I want to give you the tools and the confidence to make something of your own.
